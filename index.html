About Me

Hello, and welcome to Guidance 45! I’m Ms. Abbate, and I’ll be your instructor for this one unit transferable course. This course is perfect for those of you who have or have not taken an online class before. This course will benefit the first time college student, or the student who is ready to transfer, and everyone in between. Guidance 45 will utilize many resources from our Riverside City College Catalog as well as online resources like the CSU and UC transfer patterns. And, the best news is, you won’t have to purchase any textbooks for this course; everything that we utilize for this course can be found online.
